About Laura

Laura D'Hernandez

DRE #01820066 · Licensed Since 2007 · Big Block Powerhouse Realty

Laura De Hernandez, known as Tu Casa Con Laura, is a licensed California REALTOR® with nearly two decades of experience guiding buyers, sellers and investors with clarity and care.

Based in Long Beach, she proudly serves Long Beach, South Gate, Huntington Park, Los Angeles and Compton, while working throughout Los Angeles, Riverside and San Bernardino counties.

She specializes in first-time homebuyers, divorce-related home sales, short sales, probate, down payment assistance, multifamily opportunities, commercial real estate and business opportunities. Bilingual in English and Spanish.
